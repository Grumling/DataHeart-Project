Welcome to DataHeart!


an interstellar adventure like no other! Embark on a cosmic journey where exploration, resource gathering, and ingenious building mechanics merge into a thrilling rogue-lite experience.

Set amidst the infinite vastness of space, your mission is to harness the power of data to uncover the secrets of the universe.

Traverse treacherous planets, asteroid fields and delve into mysterious space stations, all while battling formidable foes and navigating perilous terrain.

Collect valuable resources, upgrade your spacecraft, and unlock an array of breathtaking technologies to fortify your presence among the stars.

Whether you choose to embrace the role of a cunning explorer, a master builder, or a relentless warrior, the destiny of the cosmos lies within your grasp.

Will you seize the opportunity to reshape the very fabric of space itself, or will you succumb to the formidable challenges that await?
Embark on your cosmic odyssey and let DataHeart guide your path through the celestial wonders that lie ahead.

Adventure awaits!
